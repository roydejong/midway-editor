This is my story, y'all

It's all about how, when my life got turned upside down, I said "fuck it — let's build some shit".

Let's face it. Sometimes life deals you a shit hand. Things don't work out the way you wanted them to. Sometimes things are within your control, but often they are not. And when that happens, you're given a chance. Are you going to sit around and feel sorry for yourself? Or are you going to get up and do something to make it better?

The answer to that question seems so fucking simple. Get up! Do something about it! Make something of yourself! Find a way to be happy!

But when you feel heartbroken, sad, betrayed and lost sometimes it's easier to just stay in bed and sleep.
